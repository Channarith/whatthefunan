================================================================================
NOTES FOR AI ART GENERATION
================================================================================

1. Always add "child-friendly, appropriate for all ages" to prompts
2. Avoid any violent or scary imagery
3. Keep cultural elements respectful and celebratory
4. Buddha images should NOT be generated (legal compliance)
5. No Thai royal imagery (legal compliance)
6. Focus on MYTHOLOGICAL creatures, not religious figures
7. Use "Fortnite style" consistently for cohesive look
8. Add "game art, game character, video game asset" for cleaner results

STYLE KEYWORDS TO INCLUDE:
- fortnite style
- cute stylized 3d
- vibrant colors
- soft lighting
- game character art
- octane render / unreal engine 5
- full body portrait
- character concept art

AVOID:
- realistic
- photorealistic
- scary
- horror
- violent
- religious symbols
- Buddha statues
- royal figures
